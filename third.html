Bacon Cheeseburger

Where can you get a juicy All-American Bacon Cheeseburger that is superior to fast food restaurants?
Right in you backyard with this delicious recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds 80/20 ground beef
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 6 to 8 dashes hot sauce
  • 4 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • kaiser rolls
  • softened butter, for spreading, plus 2 tablespoons
  • slices of Cheddar
  • sliced cooked bacon, halved

Recipe:

  1. Place the beef into a medium mixing bowl.
    Add the cream, hot sauce, Worcestershire, salt and pepper.
    With clean hands, mix the meat well. Set aside.


  2. Next, slice the rolls in half and spread each half with some softened butter.
    Place the rolls face-down on a skillet over medium heat.
    Lightly grill the rolls.


  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add the butter.
    Form the beef mixture into patties and place in the skillet


  4. Allow each to cook about 4 minutes on the first side,
    flip them and place a slice of Cheddar on to melt.


  5. When the patties are done in the middle, place them on the base of the halved buns.
    Top each with 2 pieces of the bacon and top with the other half of the rolls.
